Mobile Applications

Healthcare is being transformed by Mobile Applications and it is estimated that more than 75% of doctors now use a smartphone at work and over 50% of the UK population own a smartphone with an expectation of a rise to 75% by 2016.

Clinical Software Solutions has medics as founders of the company providing a unique insight into the requirements of medical applications. Each project has a medic overseeing the design and outcome. Producing apps that deliver.

Using our knowledge of the market we design a solution your users will love to use.

This extra level of insight beyond a pure development company results in fewer post-deployment changes and the reduction in the total cost of delivery.

Clinical Software Solutions has a number of it's own apps that are available on the Apple iTunes and Google Play stores. These are also featured on the app gallery page.
